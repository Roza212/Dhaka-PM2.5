# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## Hyper-Local Dhaka PM2.5 STGCN

### 1. Product Vision
To architect and deploy a secure, production-ready platform for hyper-local PM2.5 prediction in Dhaka. The system forecasts localized air pollution spikes up to 6 hours in advance at specific intersections by modeling the city's traffic grid and physical flow as a mathematical graph.

### 2. Target Audience
- Smart City Planners
- Environmental Engineers
- Public Health Officials
- Academic Researchers

### 3. Core Features
- **Dynamic Predictive Mapping**: An advanced GIS dashboard anticipating PM2.5 levels based on real-time traffic bottlenecks.
- **Hyper-Local Forecasting**: Predictions up to 6 hours in advance at specific intersections.
- **Physical Flow Modeling**: Maps pollution spread along road networks and wind corridors, rather than simple radial dispersion.

### 4. Novelty & Impact
- Maps the actual physical flow of the city using graph mathematics.
- Directly outperforms standard time-series regression models.
- Produces a highly accurate, publishable geospatial AI solution tailored for smart city planning.
